Oceans Infinity
UI Designer
June 2018 - September 2018

About the Game:

Ocean Infinity is a game where the player sneak around a bank to steal the most amount of money. Made for Microsoft's Mixer, the player controls Gaston, the thief running around the bank. Viewers play as nanobots and help Gaston in his endeavors in opening doors, creating explosions to distract the guards, and more. Though whether or not these nanobots listen to Gaston helps create the chaos and success in stealing the most amount of money.
